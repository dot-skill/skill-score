# Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation harassment-free regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

Be respectful, evidence-oriented, and open to correction. Do not harass, threaten, publish private
information, or manipulate evaluations for personal advantage. Maintainers may remove content or
temporarily or permanently exclude participants for unacceptable behavior.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the contact options on their
GitHub profile. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. This policy is adapted from
the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
