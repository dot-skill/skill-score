# Governance

The project uses maintainer consensus during v0.1. Bharat Dudeja is the initial maintainer.
Protocol decisions are made in public issues and pull requests. Material scoring changes require:

1. a documented problem and alternatives;
2. anti-gaming and backwards-compatibility analysis;
3. fixtures demonstrating expected effects;
4. at least one maintainer approval; and
5. a new formula version when outputs or semantics change.

No vendor receives privileged weights or evidence status. Governance will evolve toward multiple
maintainers as independent contributors emerge. Appeals concern implementation errors and evidence
classification; they do not imply a right to a particular score.
